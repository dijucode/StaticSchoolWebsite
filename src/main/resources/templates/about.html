About Us

Founded in 2002, Little Flower School has been dedicated to providing a nurturing and comprehensive educational experience for students from Kindergarten to Grade 8. Our school is committed to shaping students into well-rounded individuals by honing their physical, social, and intellectual abilities. We follow a student-centric approach where teachers facilitate an active and engaging learning environment.

Meet Our Director: Mr. Dilip Kumar Srivastava

Mr. Dilip Kumar Srivastava is a highly respected Senior Advocate at the Civil Court with a remarkable career in law, demonstrating exceptional leadership in the legal community. Alongside his successful career, Mr. Srivastava has proven to be an accomplished entrepreneur and visionary leader. Under his guidance, Little Flower School has grown into a prestigious institution, ensuring that students not only receive a quality education but are also imbued with values that prepare them for future success.

Our mission at Little Flower School is to cultivate a nurturing and inclusive environment that inspires each student to realize their full potential.
